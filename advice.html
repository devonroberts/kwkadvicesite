What to do the Night Before

Not sure how to prepare for camp? Follow this list to make sure you've got everything you need to succeed!

  • charge your laptop (KWK provides laptops, but if you have your own, feel free to bring it)
  • plan your route (you don't want to be late on your first day!)
  • bring a reusable water bottle
  • bring a notebook, or an electronic notebook (you're not required to take notes, but you never know what you might want to jot down)
  • bring a good attitude (you're embarking on two dense weeks of learning, keep an open mind!
